How to make Umm Ali

tasty and simple

With this simple recipe you can make Umm Ali at home by yourself

Learn more

Ingredients

  • 2 slice of bread
  • cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons of sugar
  • 2 boxes of milk or 500ml
  • raisins
  • roasted walnuts

Materials

  • Knife
  • Pan
  • Stove
  • Spoon

Step

  • pour milk into the pot and heat it on the stove
  • add 2 teaspoons of sugar and cinamon into the heated milk
  • after boiling, let stand for a few minutes
  • cut 2 slice of bread and put them in a glass while waiting for the milk to cool a bit
  • sprinkle raisins and roasted walnuts over the bread
  • pour the milk onto the bread
  • chill umm ali in the refrigerator for a few minutes
  • take out your umm ali from the refrigerator
  • your umm ali is ready to serve
